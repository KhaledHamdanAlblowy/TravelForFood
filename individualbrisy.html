Brisbane foodie guide: where to go for food and stay in the center

When it comes to brisbane there is nothing better than tasting modern food. There is one of the best restuarnts that provide modern food

Bacchus:

Multi-award winning restaurant Bacchus offers outstanding modern Australian creations with superior service. It is a nice restuarnt that definetly should be visited. they serve a quality selection of beef cuts and lots and lots of courses that offer different flavours and colors. So next time you come to brisbane make sure you come by to try some of the nice food they have to offer you.

Bacchus Address

Crn Grey and Glenelg Streets, South Bank 4101 Australia
